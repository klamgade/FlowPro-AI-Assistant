# Service Offer

## Service Name

AI Work Assistant Setup

---

## Service Description

AI Work Assistant Setup is a guided 90–120 minute session designed to help SMB owners and consultants start using AI as a practical daily work assistant.

The session focuses on simple, repeatable workflows that improve productivity in common knowledge work tasks.

The goal is to help clients leave the session with **immediately usable AI workflows**, not theoretical knowledge.

---

## Who This Service Is For

This service is ideal for:

- consultants
- coaches
- agency owners
- accountants
- mortgage brokers
- small business owners
- founders
- professionals who regularly write, read, research, and attend meetings

Typical characteristics:

- curious about AI
- limited technical background
- overwhelmed by AI tools
- want practical usage rather than theory

---

## Problems This Service Solves

Many professionals experience:

- spending too much time writing emails
- difficulty summarising long documents
- inefficient research processes
- messy meeting notes and unclear actions
- lack of structured thinking support

This service introduces simple AI workflows that address these problems.

---

## Core Workflows Covered

The session focuses on five key workflows.

### Email Assistant

Helps draft, rewrite, shorten, or improve emails.

Use cases:

- replying to clients
- follow-up emails
- rewriting messy drafts
- professional tone improvements

---

### Document Summary Assistant

Helps extract key insights from long documents.

Use cases:

- reports
- proposals
- long email threads
- contracts
- technical documents

---

### Research Assistant

Helps quickly understand topics and evaluate options.

Use cases:

- market research
- comparing tools
- understanding new concepts
- exploring opportunities

---

### Meeting Summary Assistant

Converts rough notes into structured outputs.

Use cases:

- meeting summaries
- action lists
- follow-up messages
- decision tracking

---

### Brainstorming Assistant

Helps structure thinking and generate ideas.

Use cases:

- marketing ideas
- business improvements
- content planning
- strategic thinking

---

## Session Structure

Typical session flow:

1. Introduction to AI as a work assistant
2. Overview of the AI workspace
3. Demonstration of key workflows
4. Application to the client’s real work
5. Customisation of prompts and workflows
6. Wrap-up and next steps

---

## Client Outcome

After the session, the client should have:

- clear understanding of how to use AI in daily work
- 2–5 practical AI workflows tailored to their needs
- reusable prompts for common tasks
- examples relevant to their work
- confidence using AI as a thinking partner

---

## What This Service Is Not

This service does not include:

- automation setup
- system integrations
- AI agent development
- custom software development
- complex technical configurations

Those may be future services.

---

## Strategic Purpose of This Service

This service serves as an **entry point into AI services**.

It allows clients to:

- start using AI safely
- experience immediate value
- identify further opportunities for improvement

It also allows the business to:

- build relationships with clients
- understand real workflow problems
- identify opportunities for deeper AI solutions.

---

## Success Criteria

The service is successful when:

- the client immediately uses at least one workflow
- the client feels confident using AI
- the workflows match the client’s daily work
- the session feels practical and simple
- the client would recommend the service to others
