# Business Info

## Business Context

This workspace supports the development of a practical AI services offering focused on helping small and medium businesses adopt AI in everyday work.

The current service being built is:

**AI Work Assistant Setup**

A 90–120 minute guided session where a business owner or consultant learns how to use AI as a practical daily work assistant.

This is an entry-level service designed to deliver immediate value and introduce clients to AI-powered workflows.

---

## Service Objective

Help SMB owners and consultants use AI to improve productivity in common knowledge work tasks such as:

- writing emails
- summarising documents
- performing research
- organising meeting notes
- brainstorming ideas

The focus is not technical AI implementation.

The focus is **practical adoption and real business usefulness**.

---

## Target Clients

The ideal clients for this service are:

- consultants
- agency owners
- coaches
- accountants
- mortgage brokers
- service business owners
- founders of small businesses
- knowledge workers who deal with writing, documents, research, and meetings

These clients typically:

- are curious about AI
- have used ChatGPT occasionally
- want practical workflows
- do not want to learn complex tools

---

## Value Proposition

The value delivered in the session is:

- practical AI usage tailored to the client’s work
- clear workflows they can use immediately
- structured prompts for common tasks
- guidance on how to think with AI as a work assistant

The outcome is that the client leaves the session with a **personal AI workflow system** for daily work.

---

## Core Offer

Service Name:

AI Work Assistant Setup

Session Length:

90–120 minutes

Delivery Format:

Live guided session (online or in-person)

Session Structure:

1. Quick introduction to AI as a work assistant
2. Setup of a practical AI workspace
3. Demonstration of key workflows
4. Customisation of workflows for the client’s work
5. Guidance on how to continue using AI after the session

---

## Core Workflows

The service focuses on five core workflows.

1. Email Assistant  
Helps draft, rewrite, shorten, or improve emails.

2. Document Summary Assistant  
Helps extract key information from long documents or reports.

3. Research Assistant  
Helps quickly understand topics, compare options, and explore ideas.

4. Meeting Summary Assistant  
Turns messy notes or transcripts into structured summaries and action items.

5. Brainstorming Assistant  
Helps generate ideas, evaluate options, and structure thinking.

These workflows form the backbone of the service.

---

## Tools Used

The current stack for the service is intentionally simple.

Primary tools:

- Claude
- Claude Code (VS Code)
- optional ChatGPT comparison

Supporting tools:

- Google Docs or Notion for storing prompt packs
- local workspace folders for assets and examples

Automation platforms and integrations are **not part of this initial service**.

---

## Business Strategy (Current Phase)

The current phase is **service validation**.

Goals:

- build the system personally first
- refine the five core workflows
- create a repeatable session structure
- test the service with pilot clients
- collect feedback and refine the offer

The focus is **learning through real usage**, not theoretical design.

---

## Future Opportunities

If the initial service proves useful, it may lead to additional services such as:

- AI workflow optimisation
- automation setup
- AI tool stack consulting
- team AI enablement
- internal knowledge assistants
- AI-powered process improvements

However, these are **future possibilities** and not the current focus.

---

## Current Operating Principle

This workspace prioritises:

- practical usefulness
- simplicity
- speed of testing
- real-world examples
- repeatable workflows

The aim is to help business owners **actually use AI in daily work**, not just learn about AI.
